RAG & Knowledge Base Chatbots

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbots that answer questions using your own documents, databases, and knowledge — not hallucinated responses. Powered by Vertex AI and OpenAI.

Your Documents, Your Answers

Ingest PDFs, Word docs, web pages, databases, and APIs into a vector store. The chatbot retrieves relevant content and cites sources in every response.

Vertex AI + OpenAI Backend

We use Vertex AI's matching engine for vector similarity search and OpenAI or Gemini as the language model — giving you the best of both worlds.

Always Up-to-Date

New documents are automatically processed and indexed via Cloud Storage triggers and Pub/Sub, keeping your chatbot's knowledge current without manual updates.
